# PAI Inbox

> Capture quickly, classify later, retain selectively.

The inbox is a temporary holding place for ideas that may belong somewhere else later.

## Fast Capture

In a PAI conversation, use:

```text
PAI inbox: idea or thought
```

The assistant should append it to this file under `## Ideas` with today's date.

The local PAI website also has a one-line Quick Capture box. Enter the thought and press `Add idea`.

## What Belongs Here

- Ideas for improving PAI.
- Possible skills or workflows.
- Thoughts worth reconsidering.
- Links or sources that may be useful later.
- Context that might deserve a permanent home but is not ready yet.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Active tasks or commitments; use `work/active/`.
- Confirmed decisions, lessons, or preferences; use `memory/`.
- Established background; use `background/`.
- Full documents, transcripts, or large source material.
- Anything that requires immediate action.

## Entry Format

```md
- [ ] YYYY-MM-DD | One concise thought or link
```

Do not require a category during capture. Classification happens during review.

## Weekly Triage

Review every unchecked idea and choose one outcome:

- **Move** it to `assistant/`, `background/`, `memory/`, `skills/`, or `work/active/`.
- **Keep** it in the inbox when it still needs time.
- **Merge** it with a related idea.
- **Discard** it when it no longer matters.

Remove ideas after they are moved, merged, or discarded. The inbox should stay short and should approach empty after review.

Ideas that remain unchecked for 14 days are stale and should receive an explicit decision during the weekly review.
