## User Context

- Who are you, in two or three lines? (Role, stage of life, what you build or work on.)
- What do you want from a personal AI system? (e.g., "small, clear, easy to grow" vs. "comprehensive from day one".)
- What should the assistant know about how you like systems to feel? (Practical vs. abstract, minimal vs. rich.)

## Working Domains

List the areas where you most often want help, for example:

- A professional field or two
- Research, briefings, and writing
- Personal projects and data workflows
- Documents, spreadsheets, and presentation artifacts

## Naming Guidance

- If you want the assistant to use a name for itself or for you, state it here.
- If a name is needed and none is given, the assistant should ask instead of assuming one.
